3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  1. Provide the Service: Deliver real-time gas fee data, manage your account, enforce rate limits, and process API requests.
  2. Process payments: Manage subscriptions, billing cycles, and plan upgrades/downgrades through Square.
  3. Send communications: Transactional emails (account confirmation, billing receipts, service alerts) and, with your consent, product updates.
  4. Improve the Service: Analyze usage patterns, diagnose issues, and develop new features.
  5. Ensure security: Detect abuse, enforce terms of service, and protect against unauthorized access.
  6. Comply with legal obligations: Respond to lawful requests from authorities and maintain required records.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, we process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)): Processing necessary to provide the Service you signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): Service improvement, security, and fraud prevention.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): Optional marketing communications — you may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)): Tax and accounting record-keeping.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Restriction: Request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent: Opt out of marketing communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@feescout.bond. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to individuals under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States. If you are located outside the U.S., we rely on appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) to protect your data during transfer.

10. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Password hashing (bcrypt)
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • API key authentication with rate limiting
  • Access controls limiting who can view user data
  • Regular security reviews

No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Do Not Track Signals

Our Service does not currently respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals because no uniform standard has been adopted. We will update this policy if a standard is established.

12. Changes to This Policy
