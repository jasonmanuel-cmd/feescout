Frequently Asked Questions

How does FeeScout work?

FeeScout pulls real-time gas fee data from Blockchair's API across 41+ blockchains. We normalize everything to USD so you can compare fees across chains at a glance.

What's included in the free tier?

Free accounts get 100 API calls per day, access to the top 5 cheapest chains, and data refreshed every 5 minutes. No credit card required.

How do I use the API?

Sign up for a free account, grab your API key from the dashboard, and add it to your requests: curl -H "X-API-Key: fs_your_key" https://feescout.com/api/gas-fees/latest

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, cancel anytime from your dashboard. No questions asked. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

Which blockchains do you support?

We track 41+ chains including Ethereum, Polygon, Solana, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, Avalanche, BNB Chain, Fantom, Bitcoin, and many more.
