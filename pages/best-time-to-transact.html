Why Gas Fees Fluctuate

Gas fees are driven by supply and demand:

  • Network congestion: More transactions = higher fees
  • Time of day: US business hours are most expensive
  • Day of week: Weekends are cheapest
  • Special events: NFT drops, token launches, and DeFi events cause spikes
  • MEV activity: Bot-driven transactions can inflate fees
